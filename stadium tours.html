Choose Your Experience

The Stadium Tour

Our most popular tour, led by our expert guides.

  • Walk down the Players' Tunnel and feel the roar of 74,000 fans.
  • Sit in the Manager's seat in the home dugout.
  • Explore the Directors' Lounge and the prestigious Pitchside.
  • Visit the Home Team Dressing Room (subject to availability on event days).
  • See the player interview zone and the Academy exhibition.

The Stadium Tour & Museum Entry

The complete United experience. Includes the guided Stadium Tour plus entry to our award-winning Museum.

  • Marvel at the Treble-winning trophies of 1999.
  • Pay respects at the Munich Air Disaster memorial.
  • See iconic memorabilia from Best, Charlton, Robson, Cantona, Ronaldo, and modern heroes.
  • Interactive exhibits and the chance to lift the Premier League trophy.

VIP Legend Experience

An exclusive small-group tour hosted by a Manchester United Legend.

  • Enjoy a personal Q&A session and photo opportunity with a Club Legend.
  • Extended, in-depth access to restricted areas.
  • Includes a complimentary drink and a unique gift.
  • Limited availability – book well in advance.

Self-Guided Museum & Trophy Viewing

Perfect for those short on time. Explore the Museum and see the trophy collection at your own pace, with optional multimedia guides.
